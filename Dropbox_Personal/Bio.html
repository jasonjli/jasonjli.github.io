A Short Bio


Dr. Jason Jingshi Li is a Watson Senior Developer at IBM in Canberra, Australia. He has previously held a range of roles across academia and industry, including Tech Lead Software Engineer at Shoretel (NASDAQ:SHOR), Research Fellow at the Research School of Computer Science in the Australian National University (ANU), and Postdoctoral Researcher (Collaborateur Scientifique) at the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory in the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology at Lausanne (EPFL). He received a PhD in computer science from the Australian National University. His work in AI research integrates theory and models in spatial reasoning, machine learning and computational game theory to develop efficient and intelligent systems in real and artificial environments. His research findings have been published at leading research journals and conferences proceedings such as Artificial Intelligence Journal, IEEE Transaction on Computers, AAAI, IJCAI, KR, etc. His research has been supported by the Swiss National Science Foundation, the Australian Research Council and National ICT Australia Ltd. He is a member of IEEE and AAAI, and he has served on the program committees of the International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence (IJCAI) and the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI).
