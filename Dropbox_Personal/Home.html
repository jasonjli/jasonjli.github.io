About Me


I am currently Watson Senior Developer at IBM in Canberra Australia. I have previously held a range of roles across academia and industry, including Tech Lead Software Engineer at Shoretel (NASDAQ:SHOR), Research Fellow at the Research School of Computer Science in the Australian National University (ANU), and Postdoctoral Researcher (Collaborateur Scientifique) at the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory in the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology at Lausanne (EPFL). I received a PhD in computer science from the Australian National University.


My research interests are in Artificial Intelligence, and more specifically creating systems that understand and reason with spatial information in order to emulate intelligent behaviour.
